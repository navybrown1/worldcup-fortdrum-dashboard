Travel insights from Fort Drum

The blunt version. This is the part that helps you decide whether a venue is a same-day play, a weekend trip, or a real flight commitment.

Toronto is the high-value quick strike

It is the closest venue in this set, sits around a four-hour drive from Fort Drum, and gives you a real international World Cup trip without turning it into a full expedition. The catch is the border, not the mileage.

MetLife is the cleanest U.S.-only play

It is not the absolute closest, but it is a straightforward domestic trip with eight matches including the final. If you want the biggest upside without leaving the U.S., this is the move.

Philadelphia is still realistic

It is a longer drive than MetLife, but still road-trip territory. It gives you six matches and can make sense if the fixture you want lands there instead of New Jersey.

Miami, Vancouver, and Mexico City are flight trips

These are not “maybe I’ll just drive it” options unless you enjoy suffering. Miami is the easiest of the three by air from Syracuse. Vancouver and Mexico City are worth it for marquee value, but they are real travel days.
